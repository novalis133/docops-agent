# Employee Expense Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** January 10, 2026
**Policy Owner:** Finance Department

## Purpose

This policy outlines procedures for submitting and approving business-related expenses.

## Eligible Expenses

The company reimburses reasonable expenses incurred for business purposes:
- Travel (airfare, hotels, ground transportation)
- Meals during business travel
- Client entertainment
- Professional development
- Office supplies for remote workers

## Spending Limits

| Category | Limit | Approval Required |
|----------|-------|-------------------|
| Meals | $75/day | None |
| Hotels | $250/night | None |
| Flights | $1,500 | None |
| Equipment | $500 | Manager |
| Training | $2,500 | Manager |
| Client Entertainment | $500 | None |

## Approval Process

### Standard Expenses (Under $1,000)
Expenses under $1,000 require only **manager approval**. Submit through the expense system with:
- Receipt or invoice
- Business justification
- Cost center code

Managers should approve or reject within 3 business days.

### Large Expenses ($1,000 - $5,000)
Expenses between $1,000 and $5,000 require **manager approval only**, but must include:
- Detailed business case
- Comparison of alternatives
- Expected business benefit

### Major Expenses (Over $5,000)
Expenses over $5,000 require **manager approval**, followed by review from Finance. The Finance review is for budget tracking only and does not constitute an approval requirement.

## Pre-Approval

Pre-approval is encouraged but **not required** for any expense category. Employees may incur expenses and submit for reimbursement afterward.

## Submission Timeline

- Submit expenses within 30 days of incurrence
- Late submissions may be rejected
- International expenses may take additional processing time

## Reimbursement

Approved expenses are reimbursed via:
- Direct deposit (within 10 business days)
- Next payroll cycle

## Policy Violations

Submitting false or inflated expenses will result in:
- Denial of reimbursement
- Repayment of improperly claimed amounts
- Disciplinary action
