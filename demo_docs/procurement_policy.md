# Procurement and Purchasing Policy

**Effective Date:** November 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** November 20, 2025
**Policy Owner:** Procurement Department

## Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for purchasing goods and services to ensure fiscal responsibility and compliance.

## Scope

This policy applies to all purchases made on behalf of the company, including:
- Equipment and supplies
- Software and subscriptions
- Professional services
- Training and conferences
- Travel arrangements

## Approval Authority

### Approval Requirements by Amount

| Purchase Amount | Required Approvals |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| $0 - $500 | None required |
| $500 - $2,500 | Manager + Department Head |
| $2,500 - $10,000 | Manager + Department Head + Director |
| $10,000 - $50,000 | Manager + Director + VP |
| Over $50,000 | Manager + Director + VP + CFO |

**All approvals must be obtained BEFORE the purchase is made or expense is incurred.**

### Training and Conference Expenses

For training, conferences, and professional development:
- Under $1,000: **Director approval required**
- $1,000 - $5,000: **VP approval required**
- Over $5,000: **CFO approval required**

This applies regardless of what other policies may state.

### Travel Expenses

All travel arrangements over $500 require **pre-approval from the Director level or above**, including:
- Flights
- Hotels
- Car rentals
- Conference registrations

## Preferred Vendors

Employees must use preferred vendors when available:
- Office supplies: Staples Business Advantage
- IT equipment: Dell, Apple (via IT)
- Travel: Corporate travel portal
- Software: Approved software list

Purchases from non-preferred vendors require written justification.

## Purchase Orders

All purchases over $1,000 require a purchase order:
1. Submit request in procurement system
2. Obtain required approvals
3. Receive PO number
4. Proceed with purchase

**Do not make purchases over $1,000 without a PO number.**

## Emergency Purchases

Emergency purchases may bypass normal approval chains if:
- Business continuity is at risk
- Delay would cause significant harm
- VP or above provides verbal approval

Emergency purchases must be documented within 24 hours.

## Vendor Relationships

Employees may not:
- Accept gifts over $50 from vendors
- Have personal financial relationships with vendors
- Make purchasing decisions that benefit personal interests

## Contract Requirements

Any purchase involving a contract requires:
- Legal review (contracts over $10,000)
- Security review (for IT/software)
- Insurance verification (for services)

## Policy Enforcement

Purchases made without proper approval:
- May not be reimbursed
- Will require retroactive approval from one level higher
- Repeated violations may result in disciplinary action

## Exceptions

Exceptions to this policy require written approval from the CFO or CEO.
